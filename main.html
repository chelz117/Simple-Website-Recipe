Dishes

  • Adobo - Loved by Filipinos, made from pork/chicken/vegetables with soy sauce!
  • Kaldereta - A savory stew made with meat, tomato sauce, and vegetables.
  • Menudo - Mixed with pork, liver, potatoes, and carrots in a tomato-based sauce.
  • Afritada - Chicken or pork mixed with tomato sauce, carrots, and bell peppers.
  • Add Yours - Share your recipe idea with us!

Desserts

  • Halo-Halo - A colorful mix of shaved ice, milk, and various sweet toppings.
  • Leche Flan - Creamy caramel custard dessert.
  • Ube - Made with sweet purple yam, perfect for desserts and pastries.
  • Minatamis na Saging - Sweetened bananas in syrup.
  • Mais con Yelo - A refreshing mix of sweet corn, milk, and crushed ice.
